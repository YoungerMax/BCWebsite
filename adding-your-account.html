About the data


What is this data used for?

This data is not sold or stored on any of our servers.

This data is used to prevent the "Invalid Session" errors that require you to restart your game and the launcher. As soon as you get the invalid session error, Breeze Client gives you the option to create a new session and reconnect to the server.


We only store the encrypted versions your login credentials on your computer.

Why does Breeze Client need this data?

Minecraft sessions can only be created once authenticated with your email (or username) and password. We cannot create a new session without this information.
